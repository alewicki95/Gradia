Beautify Screenshots

Transform your ordinary screenshots into eye-catching visuals perfect for sharing. With customizable framing options and professional styling features, your screenshots will stand out from the crowd.

  • Solid, Gradient and Image framing options
  • Shadow intensity Slider
  • Customize aspect ratios
  • Make it ready for social media
  • One-click export in multiple formats

Annotate it All

Express yourself with a comprehensive set of annotation tools. Whether you need to highlight important information, add explanatory text, or blur sensitive content, Gradia has you covered. From precise drawing tools to smart blur effects, every tool is designed for both power and ease of use.

Showcase Your Code

Use the Source Snippets feature to make your code showcase-ready. Choose between six themes with both light and dark mode support, you can even give your snippet a title!

Native Integration

Built from the ground up for the modern Linux desktop. Gradia seamlessly integrates with your GNOME workflow, offering multiple ways to capture and import images while maintaining the speed and reliability you expect.

  • Seamless GNOME desktop integration
  • Wayland first approach
  • System-wide screenshot shortcuts
  • 5+ ways to quickly import images
